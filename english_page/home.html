Website Introduction

In this website, informations which returnees may be interested are gathered based on the interview made to actual dormitory students. By getting to know more about the dormitory we hope to solve returnees’ worries and doubts and that it will motivate returnees to prepare for the exam.

Please contact wesedaryo@hoge.waseda.jp, if you have any additional information needed.
